<h3 class="wp-block-heading"><strong>Good to Know Before Buying</strong></h3>
<!-- /wp:heading -->

<!-- wp:list -->
<ul class="wp-block-list"><!-- wp:list-item -->
<li>ONVIF allows cross-brand compatibility with NVRs and software</li>
<!-- /wp:list-item -->

<!-- wp:list-item -->
<li>Most smart detection happens inside the camera, not the NVR</li>
<!-- /wp:list-item -->

<!-- wp:list-item -->
<li>PoE = one cable for power + video (simpler and more reliable)</li>
<!-- /wp:list-item -->

<!-- wp:list-item -->
<li>ONVIF cameras are ideal for DIY and scalable systems</li>
<!-- /wp:list-item --></ul>
<!-- /wp:list -->

<!-- wp:heading -->
<h2 class="wp-block-heading"><strong>How to Choose the Right ONVIF Camera</strong></h2>
<!-- /wp:heading -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p><strong>What ONVIF profile do you need?</strong></p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:list -->
<ul class="wp-block-list"><!-- wp:list-item -->
<li>Profile S → basic streaming and compatibility</li>
<!-- /wp:list-item -->

<!-- wp:list-item -->
<li>Profile T → better compression and modern features</li>
<!-- /wp:list-item --></ul>
<!-- /wp:list -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p><strong>Is 4K worth it?</strong></p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:list -->
<ul class="wp-block-list"><!-- wp:list-item -->
<li>Yes if you want to zoom into faces or plates</li>
<!-- /wp:list-item -->

<!-- wp:list-item -->
<li>4MP is cheaper but loses detail quickly</li>
<!-- /wp:list-item --></ul>
<!-- /wp:list -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p><strong>Do smart features matter?</strong></p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:list -->
<ul class="wp-block-list"><!-- wp:list-item -->
<li>Human/vehicle detection reduces false alerts</li>
<!-- /wp:list-item -->

<!-- wp:list-item -->
<li>Advanced AI may not fully work across all NVRs</li>
<!-- /wp:list-item --></ul>
<!-- /wp:list -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p><strong>Lens &amp; field of view</strong></p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:list -->
<ul class="wp-block-list"><!-- wp:list-item -->
<li>Wide-angle (100°–180°) → large areas</li>
<!-- /wp:list-item -->

<!-- wp:list-item -->
<li>Varifocal/zoom → detail-focused monitoring</li>
<!-- /wp:list-item --></ul>
<!-- /wp:list -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p><strong>PoE vs Wi-Fi</strong></p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:list -->
<ul class="wp-block-list"><!-- wp:list-item -->
<li>PoE is strongly recommended for stability and reliability</li>
<!-- /wp:list-item --></ul>
<!-- /wp:list -->
