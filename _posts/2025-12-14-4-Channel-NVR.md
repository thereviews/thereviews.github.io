<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>A 4-channel NVR system is the perfect choice for small homes, apartments, and offices that need reliable security without unnecessary complexity. These systems support up to four IP cameras, offering 24/7 recording, sharp video quality, and stable performance without monthly fees. Compared to wireless cameras, 4-channel NVRs deliver smoother playback, better night vision, and uninterrupted recording even when the internet goes down. We reviewed the best 4-channel NVR systems based on video clarity, ease of installation, storage options, and overall value.</p>

<!-- wp:heading {"level":3} -->
<h3 class="wp-block-heading"><strong>How to Choose the Best 4-Channel NVR System</strong></h3>
<!-- /wp:heading -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>Choosing the right 4-channel NVR comes down to a few essential factors:</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:list -->
<ul class="wp-block-list"><!-- wp:list-item -->
<li><strong>Video Quality:</strong> Look for at least 4K or 5MP resolution to clearly capture faces and details in small spaces.</li>
<!-- /wp:list-item -->

<!-- wp:list-item -->
<li><strong>PoE Support:</strong> Built-in PoE ports simplify installation by using one cable per camera for power and data.</li>
<!-- /wp:list-item -->

<!-- wp:list-item -->
<li><strong>Storage Capacity:</strong> A 1–2 TB hard drive is usually enough for several days of continuous recording with four cameras.</li>
<!-- /wp:list-item -->

<!-- wp:list-item -->
<li><strong>Ease of Use:</strong> Plug-and-play systems with mobile apps are ideal for beginners.</li>
<!-- /wp:list-item -->

<!-- wp:list-item -->
<li><strong>Expandability:</strong> Even with four channels, make sure the system supports higher-capacity drives for longer recording history.</li>
<!-- /wp:list-item --></ul>
<!-- /wp:list -->
