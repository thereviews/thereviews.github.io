<h3 class="wp-block-heading"><strong>How to Choose the Best NVR Systems for Home</strong></h3>
<!-- /wp:heading -->

<!-- wp:table -->
<figure class="wp-block-table"><table class="has-fixed-layout"><thead><tr><th>Factor</th><th>Why it matters</th></tr></thead><tbody><tr><td>Video Resolution &amp; Real-World Clarity</td><td>True 4K (8MP) cameras with proper bitrate produce sharper images; cheap 4K often over-compresses footage. Ideal for identifying faces, packages, and vehicles.</td></tr><tr><td>Smart Detection &amp; Alerts</td><td>Local AI (person &amp; vehicle detection) is faster, more accurate, and avoids monthly fees. Basic motion detection triggers false alerts from pets, shadows, or weather.</td></tr><tr><td>Storage Capacity &amp; Retention</td><td>2 TB ≈ 7–10 days continuous 4K recording with 4 cameras. Expandable storage is crucial if you plan to add cameras or keep footage longer.</td></tr><tr><td>Wired PoE vs Wi-Fi Cameras</td><td>PoE is more stable for 24/7 recording, requires no batteries, and has less interference. Wi-Fi is easier to install but less reliable for full-home coverage.</td></tr><tr><td>Ease of Installation &amp; App Quality</td><td>Look for plug-and-play setup, intuitive mobile apps, and simple alert controls. Complex apps can ruin even the best hardware.</td></tr></tbody></table></figure>
