<h1 class="wp-block-heading"><strong>How to Choose the Right NVR Security Camera System</strong></h1>
<!-- /wp:heading -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p><strong>How many cameras do you need?</strong></p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p><strong>4 cameras →</strong> Small homes, apartments, basic coverage</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p><strong>8 cameras →</strong> The best choice for most homes and small businesses</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p><strong>16 cameras →</strong> Larger homes, offices, warehouses, and future expansion</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p><strong>32+ channels →</strong> Commercial and enterprise surveillance</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>Buying an NVR with extra channels is usually better because adding cameras later is easier than replacing the recorder.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:separator -->
<hr class="wp-block-separator has-alpha-channel-opacity"/>
<!-- /wp:separator -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p><strong>Is 4K worth it?</strong></p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>For most users, yes.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>4K (8MP) provides noticeably better detail when:</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:list -->
<ul class="wp-block-list"><!-- wp:list-item -->
<li>Identifying faces</li>
<!-- /wp:list-item -->

<!-- wp:list-item -->
<li>Reading license plates</li>
<!-- /wp:list-item -->

<!-- wp:list-item -->
<li>Zooming into recorded footage</li>
<!-- /wp:list-item --></ul>
<!-- /wp:list -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>However, higher resolution also requires:</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:list -->
<ul class="wp-block-list"><!-- wp:list-item -->
<li>More storage</li>
<!-- /wp:list-item -->

<!-- wp:list-item -->
<li>More bandwidth</li>
<!-- /wp:list-item -->

<!-- wp:list-item -->
<li>Better camera placement</li>
<!-- /wp:list-item --></ul>
<!-- /wp:list -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>A well-positioned 4K camera can outperform a poorly installed higher-resolution camera.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:separator -->
<hr class="wp-block-separator has-alpha-channel-opacity"/>
<!-- /wp:separator -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p><strong>Do AI features matter?</strong></p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>AI detection helps reduce false alerts caused by:</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:list -->
<ul class="wp-block-list"><!-- wp:list-item -->
<li>Trees</li>
<!-- /wp:list-item -->

<!-- wp:list-item -->
<li>Shadows</li>
<!-- /wp:list-item -->

<!-- wp:list-item -->
<li>Rain</li>
<!-- /wp:list-item -->

<!-- wp:list-item -->
<li>Lighting changes</li>
<!-- /wp:list-item -->

<!-- wp:list-item -->
<li>Animals</li>
<!-- /wp:list-item --></ul>
<!-- /wp:list -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>The most useful AI features are:</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:list -->
<ul class="wp-block-list"><!-- wp:list-item -->
<li>Person detection</li>
<!-- /wp:list-item -->

<!-- wp:list-item -->
<li>Vehicle detection</li>
<!-- /wp:list-item -->

<!-- wp:list-item -->
<li>Animal detection</li>
<!-- /wp:list-item --></ul>
<!-- /wp:list -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>Advanced systems may also include:</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:list -->
<ul class="wp-block-list"><!-- wp:list-item -->
<li>Face recognition</li>
<!-- /wp:list-item -->

<!-- wp:list-item -->
<li>License plate recognition</li>
<!-- /wp:list-item -->

<!-- wp:list-item -->
<li>Perimeter alerts</li>
<!-- /wp:list-item --></ul>
<!-- /wp:list -->

<!-- wp:separator -->
<hr class="wp-block-separator has-alpha-channel-opacity"/>
<!-- /wp:separator -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p><strong>How much storage is enough?</strong></p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>Storage depends on:</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:list -->
<ul class="wp-block-list"><!-- wp:list-item -->
<li>Number of cameras</li>
<!-- /wp:list-item -->

<!-- wp:list-item -->
<li>Resolution</li>
<!-- /wp:list-item -->

<!-- wp:list-item -->
<li>Recording schedule</li>
<!-- /wp:list-item -->

<!-- wp:list-item -->
<li>Retention needs</li>
<!-- /wp:list-item --></ul>
<!-- /wp:list -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>Typical examples:</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:list -->
<ul class="wp-block-list"><!-- wp:list-item -->
<li>2TB → Good for many home systems</li>
<!-- /wp:list-item -->

<!-- wp:list-item -->
<li>4TB+ → Better for larger multi-camera setups</li>
<!-- /wp:list-item -->

<!-- wp:list-item -->
<li>8TB–16TB+ → Better for businesses</li>
<!-- /wp:list-item --></ul>
<!-- /wp:list -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>Expandable storage is recommended if you plan to add more cameras.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:separator -->
<hr class="wp-block-separator has-alpha-channel-opacity"/>
<!-- /wp:separator -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p><strong>PoE vs Wi-Fi</strong></p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>For serious security installations, PoE is usually the better choice.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>PoE provides:</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:list -->
<ul class="wp-block-list"><!-- wp:list-item -->
<li>Power and video through one cable</li>
<!-- /wp:list-item -->

<!-- wp:list-item -->
<li>More stable connections</li>
<!-- /wp:list-item -->

<!-- wp:list-item -->
<li>Better video reliability</li>
<!-- /wp:list-item -->

<!-- wp:list-item -->
<li>Cleaner installation</li>
<!-- /wp:list-item --></ul>
<!-- /wp:list -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>Wi-Fi cameras are useful where cables are difficult, but wired systems are generally better for long-term surveillance.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->
