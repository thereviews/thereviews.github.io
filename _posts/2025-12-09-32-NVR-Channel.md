<!-- wp:heading -->
<h2 class="wp-block-heading"><strong>How to Choose the Right 32-Channel NVR System</strong></h2>
<!-- /wp:heading -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p><strong>How many cameras do you need?</strong></p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:list -->
<ul class="wp-block-list"><!-- wp:list-item -->
<li>16–24 cameras → large homes or small businesses</li>
<!-- /wp:list-item -->

<!-- wp:list-item -->
<li>24–32 cameras → warehouses, schools, hotels, or campuses</li>
<!-- /wp:list-item -->

<!-- wp:list-item -->
<li>Always leave extra channels for future expansion</li>
<!-- /wp:list-item --></ul>
<!-- /wp:list -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p><strong>Is 4K or 12MP worth it?</strong></p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:list -->
<ul class="wp-block-list"><!-- wp:list-item -->
<li>Yes if you need to zoom into faces, license plates, or large areas</li>
<!-- /wp:list-item -->

<!-- wp:list-item -->
<li>Higher resolution means higher bandwidth and storage needs</li>
<!-- /wp:list-item --></ul>
<!-- /wp:list -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p><strong>Do AI features matter?</strong></p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:list -->
<ul class="wp-block-list"><!-- wp:list-item -->
<li>AI reduces false alerts from rain, shadows, or trees</li>
<!-- /wp:list-item -->

<!-- wp:list-item -->
<li>Person/vehicle detection is the most useful feature</li>
<!-- /wp:list-item -->

<!-- wp:list-item -->
<li>Face recognition and people counting add business value</li>
<!-- /wp:list-item --></ul>
<!-- /wp:list -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p><strong>How much storage is enough?</strong></p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:list -->
<ul class="wp-block-list"><!-- wp:list-item -->
<li>16TB ≈ ~7–10 days for 16–20 cameras at 4K (24/7)</li>
<!-- /wp:list-item -->

<!-- wp:list-item -->
<li>Look for 2–4 HDD bays with 16–32TB+ support for long retention</li>
<!-- /wp:list-item --></ul>
<!-- /wp:list -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p><strong>PoE vs non-PoE</strong></p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:list -->
<ul class="wp-block-list"><!-- wp:list-item -->
<li>Built-in PoE is strongly recommended for clean, reliable installs</li>
<!-- /wp:list-item -->

<!-- wp:list-item -->
<li>Most 32-channel NVRs include 16 PoE ports — external switches handle the rest</li>
<!-- /wp:list-item --></ul>
<!-- /wp:list -->

<!-- wp:separator -->
