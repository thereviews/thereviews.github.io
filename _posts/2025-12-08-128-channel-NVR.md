<h2 class="wp-block-heading"><strong>How to Choose the Right 128-Channel NVR</strong></h2>
<!-- /wp:heading -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p><strong>How many cameras do you really need?</strong></p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:list -->
<ul class="wp-block-list"><!-- wp:list-item -->
<li>64–96 cameras → large facilities</li>
<!-- /wp:list-item -->

<!-- wp:list-item -->
<li>96–128 cameras → campuses, hospitals, industrial sites</li>
<!-- /wp:list-item -->

<!-- wp:list-item -->
<li>Always leave expansion headroom</li>
<!-- /wp:list-item --></ul>
<!-- /wp:list -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p><strong>Is ultra-high resolution worth it?</strong></p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:list -->
<ul class="wp-block-list"><!-- wp:list-item -->
<li>12MP+ is ideal for wide areas, license plates, and forensic zoom</li>
<!-- /wp:list-item -->

<!-- wp:list-item -->
<li>4–8MP saves storage but sacrifices detail</li>
<!-- /wp:list-item --></ul>
<!-- /wp:list -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p><strong>How much storage is enough?</strong></p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:list -->
<ul class="wp-block-list"><!-- wp:list-item -->
<li>64TB = short retention at 128 channels</li>
<!-- /wp:list-item -->

<!-- wp:list-item -->
<li>128–256TB = long-term enterprise retention</li>
<!-- /wp:list-item -->

<!-- wp:list-item -->
<li>Look for 16+ drive bays with RAID support</li>
<!-- /wp:list-item --></ul>
<!-- /wp:list -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p><strong>Do enterprise AI features matter?</strong></p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:list -->
<ul class="wp-block-list"><!-- wp:list-item -->
<li>Face recognition, LPR, and people counting reduce manual review</li>
<!-- /wp:list-item -->

<!-- wp:list-item -->
<li>Smart analytics are especially valuable for security operations centers</li>
<!-- /wp:list-item --></ul>
<!-- /wp:list -->
