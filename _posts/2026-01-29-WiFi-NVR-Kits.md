<h2 class="wp-block-heading"><strong>How to Choose the Right WiFi NVR Kit</strong></h2>
<!-- /wp:heading -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p><strong>How many cameras do you need?</strong></p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:list -->
<ul class="wp-block-list"><!-- wp:list-item -->
<li>4 cameras → apartments or small homes</li>
<!-- /wp:list-item -->

<!-- wp:list-item -->
<li>8 cameras → average home or office</li>
<!-- /wp:list-item -->

<!-- wp:list-item -->
<li>16 cameras → full property coverage<br>Always leave room for future expansion.</li>
<!-- /wp:list-item --></ul>
<!-- /wp:list -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p><strong>Is higher resolution worth it?</strong></p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:list -->
<ul class="wp-block-list"><!-- wp:list-item -->
<li>5MP / 2K = strong detail for faces and vehicles</li>
<!-- /wp:list-item -->

<!-- wp:list-item -->
<li>1080p = good for general monitoring<br>Higher resolution means more storage use.</li>
<!-- /wp:list-item --></ul>
<!-- /wp:list -->

<p><strong>Do AI features matter?</strong></p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:list -->
<ul class="wp-block-list"><!-- wp:list-item -->
<li>Human detection reduces false alerts</li>
<!-- /wp:list-item -->

<!-- wp:list-item -->
<li>PIR sensors improve motion accuracy</li>
<!-- /wp:list-item -->

<!-- wp:list-item -->
<li>Custom detection zones help avoid alert spam</li>
<!-- /wp:list-item --></ul>
<!-- /wp:list -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p><strong>How much storage is enough?</strong></p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:list -->
<ul class="wp-block-list"><!-- wp:list-item -->
<li>1TB = basic home use</li>
<!-- /wp:list-item -->

<!-- wp:list-item -->
<li>2TB–3TB = better for 24/7 recording<br>Look for H.265/H.265+ compression to save space.</li>
<!-- /wp:list-item --></ul>
<!-- /wp:list -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p><strong>WiFi stability matters</strong></p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:list -->
<ul class="wp-block-list"><!-- wp:list-item -->
<li>Dual-band routers improve performance</li>
<!-- /wp:list-item -->

<!-- wp:list-item -->
<li>Cascade relay helps extend wireless range</li>
