<h3 class="wp-block-heading">How to Choose the Best AI NVR Recorder</h3>
<!-- /wp:heading -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>Choosing the right AI NVR comes down to these essential factors:</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:list -->
<ul class="wp-block-list"><!-- wp:list-item -->
<li><strong>AI Detection Performance:</strong> Look for real AI support such as human, vehicle, and face detection at the recorder level.</li>
<!-- /wp:list-item -->

<!-- wp:list-item -->
<li><strong>Channel Support:</strong> Choose 4/8/16+ channel NVRs depending on your home size and future camera expansion.</li>
<!-- /wp:list-item -->

<!-- wp:list-item -->
<li><strong>Storage &amp; Retention:</strong> A minimum of 1 HDD bay with 6–10 TB support is ideal for long recording retention.</li>
<!-- /wp:list-item -->

<!-- wp:list-item -->
<li><strong>Compatibility:</strong> ONVIF and wide IP camera support help you connect third-party cameras easily.</li>
<!-- /wp:list-item -->

<!-- wp:list-item -->
<li><strong>Software Usability:</strong> A clean UI, stable mobile apps, and remote access are key for home monitoring.</li>
<!-- /wp:list-item --></ul>
