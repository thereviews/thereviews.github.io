<h2 class="wp-block-heading"><strong>How to Choose the Right PoE NVR Security Camera System</strong></h2>
<!-- /wp:heading -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p><strong>How many PoE cameras do you need?</strong></p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>The number of cameras determines the NVR channel capacity you need.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:list -->
<ul class="wp-block-list"><!-- wp:list-item -->
<li><strong>4-channel PoE NVRs →</strong> Small homes, apartments, and basic monitoring</li>
<!-- /wp:list-item --></ul>
<!-- /wp:list -->

<!-- wp:list -->
<ul class="wp-block-list"><!-- wp:list-item -->
<li><strong>8-channel PoE NVRs →</strong> Best choice for most homes and small businesses</li>
<!-- /wp:list-item --></ul>
<!-- /wp:list -->

<!-- wp:list -->
<ul class="wp-block-list"><!-- wp:list-item -->
<li><strong>16-channel PoE NVRs →</strong> Larger homes, offices, warehouses, and expansion</li>
<!-- /wp:list-item --></ul>

<!-- wp:list -->
<ul class="wp-block-list"><!-- wp:list-item -->
<li><strong>32+ channels →</strong> Commercial and enterprise surveillance</li>
<!-- /wp:list-item --></ul>

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>It is usually better to choose an NVR with extra channels because adding PoE cameras later is easier than replacing the recorder.</p>
