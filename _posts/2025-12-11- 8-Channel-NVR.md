<!-- wp:heading {"level":3} -->
<h3 class="wp-block-heading"><strong>How to Choose the Best 8-Channel NVR System</strong></h3>
<!-- /wp:heading -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>Choosing the right 8-channel NVR involves more than just channel count. Focus on these key factors:</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p><strong>1. Video Resolution &amp; Clarity</strong></p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:list -->
<ul class="wp-block-list"><!-- wp:list-item -->
<li>4K or 5MP cameras are recommended for clear identification of faces, license plates, and critical details.</li>
<!-- /wp:list-item -->

<!-- wp:list-item -->
<li>Higher bitrates improve image clarity during motion.</li>
<!-- /wp:list-item --></ul>
<!-- /wp:list -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p><strong>2. PoE Support</strong></p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:list -->
<ul class="wp-block-list"><!-- wp:list-item -->
<li>PoE (Power over Ethernet) simplifies installation by delivering both power and data through a single cable.</li>
<!-- /wp:list-item -->

<!-- wp:list-item -->
<li>Ensures stable, continuous recording without messy power adapters.</li>
<!-- /wp:list-item --></ul>
<!-- /wp:list -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p><strong>3. Storage Capacity</strong></p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:list -->
<ul class="wp-block-list"><!-- wp:list-item -->
<li>At least <strong>2 TB</strong> recommended for multiple 4K cameras running 24/7.</li>
<!-- /wp:list-item -->

<!-- wp:list-item -->
<li>Expandable HDD support is critical if you plan to increase storage or camera count in the future.</li>
<!-- /wp:list-item --></ul>
<!-- /wp:list -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p><strong>4. Smart AI Detection</strong></p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:list -->
<ul class="wp-block-list"><!-- wp:list-item -->
<li>Person and vehicle detection reduce false alerts.</li>
<!-- /wp:list-item -->

<!-- wp:list-item -->
<li>Advanced AI features like facial recognition or heat sensing are ideal for reducing unnecessary notifications.</li>
<!-- /wp:list-item --></ul>
<!-- /wp:list -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p><strong>5. Expandability &amp; Compatibility</strong></p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:list -->
<ul class="wp-block-list"><!-- wp:list-item -->
<li>Ensure the NVR supports <strong>all 8 channels</strong> and allows future upgrades.</li>
<!-- /wp:list-item -->

<!-- wp:list-item -->
<li>ONVIF-compatible systems provide flexibility to use cameras from different brands.</li>
<!-- /wp:list-item --></ul>
<!-- /wp:list -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p><strong>6. Night Vision &amp; Outdoor Performance</strong></p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:list -->
<ul class="wp-block-list"><!-- wp:list-item -->
<li>Cameras with <strong>color night vision</strong> or IR lighting ensure visibility in dark areas.</li>
<!-- /wp:list-item -->

<!-- wp:list-item -->
<li>Weatherproof ratings (IP66/IP67) are essential for outdoor setups.</li>
<!-- /wp:list-item --></ul>
<!-- /wp:list -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>7. Ease of Use</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:list -->
<ul class="wp-block-list"><!-- wp:list-item -->
<li>Systems with <strong>plug-and-play PoE cameras</strong> and intuitive mobile apps save time.</li>
<!-- /wp:list-item -->

<!-- wp:list-item -->
<li>Consider software interface, alert customization, and remote access quality.</li>
<!-- /wp:list-item --></ul>
<!-- /wp:list -->
