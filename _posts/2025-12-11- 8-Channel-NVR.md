<h2 class="wp-block-heading"><strong>How to Choose the Best 8 Channel NVR System</strong></h2>
<!-- /wp:heading -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>When choosing an 8-channel NVR system, consider the following:</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:list -->
<ul class="wp-block-list"><!-- wp:list-item -->
<li><strong>Resolution:</strong> Look for 4K or at least 5MP to ensure clear facial and license plate identification.</li>
<!-- /wp:list-item -->

<!-- wp:list-item -->
<li><strong>PoE Support:</strong> PoE NVRs simplify installation by using a single cable for both power and data.</li>
<!-- /wp:list-item -->

<!-- wp:list-item -->
<li><strong>Storage Capacity:</strong> At least 2 TB of storage is recommended for multiple 4K cameras running 24/7.</li>
<!-- /wp:list-item -->

<!-- wp:list-item -->
<li><strong>Smart Detection:</strong> AI features such as person/vehicle detection help reduce false alerts.</li>
<!-- /wp:list-item -->

<!-- wp:list-item -->
<li><strong>Expandability:</strong> Ensure the system supports additional cameras up to all 8 channels.</li>
<!-- /wp:list-item --></ul>
<!-- /wp:list -->
