<!-- wp:heading -->
<h2 class="wp-block-heading"><strong>How to Choose the Right ONVIF NVR</strong></h2>
<!-- /wp:heading -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p><strong>How many cameras do you need?</strong></p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:list -->
<ul class="wp-block-list"><!-- wp:list-item -->
<li>4 channels → small homes or offices</li>
<!-- /wp:list-item -->

<!-- wp:list-item -->
<li>8 channels → most homes or small businesses</li>
<!-- /wp:list-item -->

<!-- wp:list-item -->
<li>16+ channels → warehouses, commercial, or large properties</li>
<!-- /wp:list-item --></ul>
<!-- /wp:list -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p><strong>Which ONVIF profiles matter?</strong></p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:list -->
<ul class="wp-block-list"><!-- wp:list-item -->
<li>Profile S = required for video streaming</li>
<!-- /wp:list-item -->

<!-- wp:list-item -->
<li>Profile T = better compression + event handling</li>
<!-- /wp:list-item -->

<!-- wp:list-item -->
<li>Profile G = storage management (nice bonus)</li>
<!-- /wp:list-item --></ul>
<!-- /wp:list -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p><strong>Is bandwidth important?</strong></p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:list -->
<ul class="wp-block-list"><!-- wp:list-item -->
<li>Yes. Higher Mbps input = smoother multi-camera recording, especially at 4K+.</li>
<!-- /wp:list-item --></ul>
<!-- /wp:list -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p><strong>Do you need PoE?</strong></p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:list -->
<ul class="wp-block-list"><!-- wp:list-item -->
<li>Not required for ONVIF, but <strong>strongly recommended</strong> for clean installs and reliability.</li>
<!-- /wp:list-item --></ul>
<!-- /wp:list -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p><strong>Hardware vs software NVR?</strong></p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:list -->
<ul class="wp-block-list"><!-- wp:list-item -->
<li>Hardware = simpler, quieter, more stable</li>
<!-- /wp:list-item -->

<!-- wp:list-item -->
<li>Software (Blue Iris) = unlimited scalability and customization</li>
<!-- /wp:list-item --></ul>
<!-- /wp:list -->
