<h2 class="wp-block-heading"><strong>How to Choose the Best IP Camera NVR</strong></h2>
<!-- /wp:heading -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>Selecting the right IP camera NVR comes down to a few essential factors:</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:list -->
<ul class="wp-block-list"><!-- wp:list-item -->
<li><strong>Camera Compatibility:</strong> Make sure the NVR supports ONVIF so it works with third-party IP cameras.</li>
<!-- /wp:list-item -->

<!-- wp:list-item -->
<li><strong>Video Resolution:</strong> Choose NVRs that support 4K or higher for sharper images and better zoom capability.</li>
<!-- /wp:list-item -->

<!-- wp:list-item -->
<li><strong>Channel Capacity:</strong> Consider how many cameras you need now and in the future—8-channel systems are ideal for homes, while larger setups suit businesses.</li>
<!-- /wp:list-item -->

<!-- wp:list-item -->
<li><strong>Storage Options:</strong> Built-in HDD support with expandability ensures continuous recording without data loss.</li>
<!-- /wp:list-item -->

<!-- wp:list-item -->
<li><strong>Ease of Use:</strong> A clean interface, mobile app access, and plug-and-play support simplify daily operation.</li>
<!-- /wp:list-item --></ul>
