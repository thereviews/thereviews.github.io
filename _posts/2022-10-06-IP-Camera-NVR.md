<!-- wp:heading {"level":3} -->
<h3 class="wp-block-heading"><strong>How to Choose the Right IP Camera NVR</strong></h3>
<!-- /wp:heading -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p><strong>How many cameras do you need?</strong></p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:list -->
<ul class="wp-block-list"><!-- wp:list-item -->
<li>4–8 cameras → most homes and small offices</li>
<!-- /wp:list-item -->

<!-- wp:list-item -->
<li>8–16+ cameras → businesses and commercial spaces</li>
<!-- /wp:list-item -->

<!-- wp:list-item -->
<li>Always leave extra channels for expansion</li>
<!-- /wp:list-item --></ul>
<!-- /wp:list -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p><strong>Is 4K (or higher) worth it?</strong></p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:list -->
<ul class="wp-block-list"><!-- wp:list-item -->
<li>Yes if you want to zoom into faces or license plates</li>
<!-- /wp:list-item -->

<!-- wp:list-item -->
<li>Lower resolutions save storage but lose usable detail</li>
<!-- /wp:list-item --></ul>
<!-- /wp:list -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p><strong>Do AI features matter?</strong></p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:list -->
<ul class="wp-block-list"><!-- wp:list-item -->
<li>AI reduces false alerts from rain, shadows, and trees</li>
<!-- /wp:list-item -->

<!-- wp:list-item -->
<li>Most NVRs rely on camera-side AI</li>
<!-- /wp:list-item --></ul>
<!-- /wp:list -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p><strong>How much storage is enough?</strong></p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:list -->
<ul class="wp-block-list"><!-- wp:list-item -->
<li>2TB ≈ ~7–10 days for 4× 4K cameras (24/7)</li>
<!-- /wp:list-item -->

<!-- wp:list-item -->
<li>Look for 8–16TB support for longer retention</li>
<!-- /wp:list-item --></ul>
<!-- /wp:list -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p><strong>PoE vs non-PoE</strong></p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:list -->
<ul class="wp-block-list"><!-- wp:list-item -->
<li>PoE is strongly recommended for clean, reliable installs</li>
<!-- /wp:list-item --></ul>
<!-- /wp:list -->
