<!-- wp:heading -->
<h2 class="wp-block-heading"><strong>How to Choose the Right 64-Channel NVR</strong></h2>
<!-- /wp:heading -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p><strong>How many cameras do you need?</strong></p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:list -->
<ul class="wp-block-list"><!-- wp:list-item -->
<li>32–48 cameras → mid-size enterprise</li>
<!-- /wp:list-item -->

<!-- wp:list-item -->
<li>48–64 cameras → large campuses or facilities</li>
<!-- /wp:list-item -->

<!-- wp:list-item -->
<li>Always leave room for future expansion</li>
<!-- /wp:list-item --></ul>
<!-- /wp:list -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p><strong>Do you need ultra-high resolution?</strong></p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:list -->
<ul class="wp-block-list"><!-- wp:list-item -->
<li>12MP+ is ideal for large areas or license plate capture</li>
<!-- /wp:list-item -->

<!-- wp:list-item -->
<li>4K (8MP) is sufficient for most enterprise deployments</li>
<!-- /wp:list-item --></ul>
<!-- /wp:list -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p><strong>Do AI features matter?</strong></p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:list -->
<ul class="wp-block-list"><!-- wp:list-item -->
<li>AI reduces false alerts and speeds investigations</li>
<!-- /wp:list-item -->

<!-- wp:list-item -->
<li>Face recognition, people counting, and perimeter detection are most useful in retail, campuses, and secured sites</li>
<!-- /wp:list-item --></ul>
<!-- /wp:list -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p><strong>How much storage is enough?</strong></p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:list -->
<ul class="wp-block-list"><!-- wp:list-item -->
<li>64 cameras @ 4K/12MP → <strong>80–128TB+</strong> recommended</li>
<!-- /wp:list-item -->

<!-- wp:list-item -->
<li>Look for <strong>8 or more HDD bays</strong> and RAID support</li>
<!-- /wp:list-item --></ul>
<!-- /wp:list -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p><strong>RAID vs non-RAID</strong></p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:list -->
<ul class="wp-block-list"><!-- wp:list-item -->
<li>RAID is strongly recommended to prevent data loss from drive failure</li>
<!-- /wp:list-item --></ul>
<!-- /wp:list -->
