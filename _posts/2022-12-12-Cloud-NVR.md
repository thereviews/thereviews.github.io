<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>Cloud NVR systems are changing how modern surveillance works by moving video storage away from local recorders and into the cloud. Instead of relying entirely on hard drives inside an <a href="https://Thereviews.github.io/NVR-security-camera">NVR</a>, footage is stored online and accessed through apps or web dashboards from virtually anywhere.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>This guide is for businesses, multi-site operators, and users who want remote access, off-site backup, and easier scalability than traditional NVR systems. We focus on what actually matters in real use: reliability, cloud storage, camera compatibility, AI features, and long-term costs.</p>
