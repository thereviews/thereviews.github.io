<h3 class="wp-block-heading"><strong>Good to Know Before Buying</strong></h3>
<!-- /wp:heading -->

<!-- wp:list -->
<ul class="wp-block-list"><!-- wp:list-item -->
<li>Cloud NVRs still record locally — the internet is only needed for remote viewing</li>
<!-- /wp:list-item -->

<!-- wp:list-item -->
<li>Most models support ONVIF, letting you mix brands</li>
<!-- /wp:list-item -->

<!-- wp:list-item -->
<li>H.265/H.265+ compression saves storage and bandwidth</li>
<!-- /wp:list-item -->

<!-- wp:list-item -->
<li>Built-in PoE makes installation much easier</li>
<!-- /wp:list-item -->

<!-- wp:list-item -->
<li>Cloud subscriptions are optional, not required</li>
<!-- /wp:list-item --></ul>

<!-- wp:heading {"level":3} -->
<h3 class="wp-block-heading"><strong>How to Choose the Right Cloud NVR</strong></h3>
<!-- /wp:heading -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p><strong>How many cameras do you need?</strong></p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:list -->
<ul class="wp-block-list"><!-- wp:list-item -->
<li>4–8 cameras → most homes</li>
<!-- /wp:list-item -->

<!-- wp:list-item -->
<li>8–16 cameras → large homes or offices</li>
<!-- /wp:list-item -->

<!-- wp:list-item -->
<li>Always leave extra channels for future expansion</li>
<!-- /wp:list-item --></ul>
<!-- /wp:list -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p><strong>Is 4K or higher worth it?</strong></p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:list -->
<ul class="wp-block-list"><!-- wp:list-item -->
<li>Yes if you want to zoom into faces or license plates</li>
<!-- /wp:list-item -->

<!-- wp:list-item -->
<li>12MP is ideal for large outdoor areas</li>
<!-- /wp:list-item --></ul>
<!-- /wp:list -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p><strong>Do AI features matter?</strong></p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:list -->
<ul class="wp-block-list"><!-- wp:list-item -->
<li>Motion detection is standard</li>
<!-- /wp:list-item -->

<!-- wp:list-item -->
<li>Advanced AI depends on camera compatibility</li>
<!-- /wp:list-item --></ul>
<!-- /wp:list -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p><strong>How much storage is enough?</strong></p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:list -->
<ul class="wp-block-list"><!-- wp:list-item -->
<li>4–8TB = ~2–4 weeks for 8–16 cameras at 4K</li>
<!-- /wp:list-item -->

<!-- wp:list-item -->
<li>Look for expandable HDD support</li>
<!-- /wp:list-item --></ul>
<!-- /wp:list -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p><strong>PoE vs non-PoE</strong></p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:list -->
<ul class="wp-block-list"><!-- wp:list-item -->
<li>PoE is strongly recommended for simpler wiring and reliability</li>
<!-- /wp:list-item --></ul>
<!-- /wp:list -->
